The given dataset contains simplified information from a dating website. In the dataset:

There are 30 men and 30 women. 
Each woman has met all the men; each men has met all the women.
Each person has a clear preference list of potential partners, rank from the most ideal partner (indexing 0) to the least ideal partner (indexing 29).  
Your task is to find a stable-matching solution based on their preferences. Keep in mind that in a correct solution, there are no two people of opposite sex who would both rather have each other than their current partners.
