Practice Project 2

In this project, you will work with a given dataset and apply the Gale–Shapley algorithm to solve a stable-matching problem.

Before you start: Download the dataset and starter script below. Place project2_data.json in the same folder as your script. Upload your completed Python script on Canvas before the deadline.

Background

The dataset contains simplified information from a dating website. There are 30 men and 30 women, each with a ranked preference list over everyone in the other group. Your task is to find a stable matching: no two people of opposite sex who would both prefer each other over their current partners.
